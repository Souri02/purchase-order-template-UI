How to use this prototype

  1. Sign in (basic user) — only Purchase Order is shown.
  2. Open Purchase Order to land directly on the PO screen.
  3. Pick a distributor, delivery date & ship-to store.
  4. Tap the grey Add Product box — choose Scan barcode or Search by name (for damaged/missing barcodes & fresh produce).
  5. Product loads from the database; set Qty, Case/Each & use + / −.
  6. A completed product turns green; a new Add Product appears.
  7. Add to order → review. Missing details show red.
  8. Submit to place the order.

Barcode scan & product lookup are simulated against a sample product database for this static demo.
